About Bais City National Science High School

Bais City National Science High School (BCNSHS) is a premier public secondary institution in Bais City, Negros Oriental, dedicated to providing quality science-oriented education to Filipino learners. Established to nurture future scientists, innovators, and leaders, BCNSHS emphasizes STEM while valuing the humanities and social responsibility.

Vision

We dream of Filipinos who deeply love their country, grow to their fullest potential, and help build the nation. As a learner-centered institution, DepEd continuously improves to serve students, teachers, and the community better.

Mission

DepEd ensures the right of every Filipino to quality, inclusive, and culture-based education by creating safe, child-friendly learning environments; guiding learners through committed teachers; enabling effective leadership and teamwork; and working with families and communities.

Core Values

Maka-Diyos — God-loving
Maka-tao — Respectful and compassionate
Makakalikasan — Caring for the environment
Makabansa — Patriotic and nation-loving
